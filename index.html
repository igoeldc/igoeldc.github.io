About Me

I am a fourth-year student at the Illinois Institute of Technology, where I am pursuing a dual degree in Applied Mathematics and Data Science, along with a Master's in Artificial Intelligence and a minor in Finance. My passion lies at the intersection of language, data, and computation. I am driven to use my skills in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and mathematical modeling to understand and solve complex problems, from preserving under-resourced languages to navigating the intricacies of financial markets. My global upbringing has given me a unique perspective on the socio-cultural impact of technology, and my fluency in multiple languages fuels my work in NLP. I am currently seeking internship opportunities where I can apply my expertise in generative AI and applied mathematics to real-world challenges.
