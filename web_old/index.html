IB Extended Essay

This is my IB Extended Essay which explores how Natural Language Processing (NLP) can help preserve minority, regional, and oppressed languages. It addresses challenges like limited data and reviews industry approaches, such as speech-based models and gamification, while examining the social, cultural, and ethical aspects of language preservation.

Erdős Co-authorship Network

Designed and implemented a co-author network to analyze the influence of research papers on contemporary and future research, leveraging weighted and unweighted adjacency matrices with Erdos1 data.

Explored Network Science principles, focusing on measuring influence, extracting relevant data, and creating influence measurement algorithms.
